Lab members

Eric Capo is Assistant Professor at the Department of Ecology and Environmental Science at Umeå University (Sweden). Eric has a PhD in molecular paleoecology with expertise in molecular biology, bioinformatics and aquatic microbial ecology. His current research focus on the consequences of deoxygenation of coastal systems on microbial communities and related ecosystem services. Eric developed the pipeline marky-coco to detect genes involved in mercury cycling from environmental metagenomes. He is the the founder and coordinator of the sedaDNA scientific society. Learn more about Eric here. Contact : eric.capo@umu.se
