Lab members

Eric Capo is Assistant Professor at the Department of Ecology and Environmental Science at Umeå University (Sweden). Eric has a PhD in molecular paleoecology with expertise in molecular biology, bioinformatics and aquatic microbial ecology. He is the the founder and coordinator of the sedaDNA scientific society.

Eric´s CV. Contact : eric.capo@umu.se

Júlia Dordal-Soriano is a PhD student at the ICM-CSIC (Barcelona). Her current research focuses on the study of microbial processes and environmental factors that control mercury transformations in the global ocean, with particular emphasis on the formation of methylmercury.
