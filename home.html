We explore the ecology of marine and freshwater systems with a focus on aquatic microorganisms i.e., bacteria, archaea, and protists. Our focus lies in understanding the temporal dynamics of microbial communities and their functional responses to environmental change, such as climate change, eutrophication, mercury pollution, or coral bleaching. We apply molecular ecology methods to water samples and sedimentary archives to unravel past and present microbial communities in aquatic ecosystems.
